# Avatar image tips

- Make the face and mouth clearly identifiable.
- Use a tight crop and minimize dead space.
- Keep the intended body framing consistent with the selected aspect ratio.
- Test the actual image; style, lighting, hair, hands, and background affect output.
- Non-human and cartoon characters are supported, but recognizable facial structure improves results.
- Lite performs best with stylized characters or close photorealistic face crops.
